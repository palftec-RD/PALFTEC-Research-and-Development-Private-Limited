OUR PRODUCTS

Banana Fiber

The Fibers are extracted from raw banana pseudo-stem (Trunk) of the tree. Banana Fiber is a durable, eco-friendly fiber extracted from the bark of banana plants. Known for its natural shine and high tensile strength, it is an ideal alternative to synthetic fibers and Suitable for weaving, non-woven materials, and home decor

Pineapple Fiber

The Fibers are extracted from raw pineapple leaves from farms. Pineapple Leaf Fiber is a strong, lightweight, and biodegradable fiber extracted from discarded pineapple leaves—an agricultural waste turned into a valuable resource.Offers a fine, lustrous texture similar to silk and Perfect for crafting, textiles, and sustainable fashion.

ABOUT US

Palftec Research & Development Pvt. Ltd.

Founded on January 25, 2022, by a visionary group of five innovative minds, Palftec is committed to creating a sustainable future by converting agricultural residues into valuable, eco-friendly products. We represent the pinnacle of sustainable innovation and environmental responsibility.

At Palftec, we’re revolutionizing the way agricultural waste is perceived by transforming what was once considered worthless—discarded pineapple leaves—into high-quality, eco-friendly fiber. Using advanced, precision-engineered machinery, we efficiently extract Pineapple Leaf Fiber (PALF), which is not only biodegradable and durable but also serves as an exceptional sustainable alternative to synthetic and petroleum-based materials.

This innovation not only helps reduce agricultural waste and environmental pollution but also opens up new possibilities in the fields of textiles, fashion, packaging, and more—contributing significantly to a cleaner, greener, and more circular economy.
